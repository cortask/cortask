# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the community
- Show empathy towards others

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Sexualized language or imagery
- Other conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to [info@cortask.com](mailto:info@cortask.com). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers who do not follow the Code of Conduct may be removed from the project.
